## Daily Maintenance Practice

### Morning Check-in (15-20 minutes)
Begin each day by creating a quiet space for reflection. Have your journal ready and follow these steps:

1. Center yourself with three deep breaths
2. Body scan for feelings
   - Notice any physical sensations
   - Name the emotions present
   - Rate their intensity (1-10)

3. Needs Assessment
Write responses to:
   - "What needs feel most alive in me today?"
   - "What needs might need extra attention?"
   - "How might I meet these needs today?"

4. Judgment Translation Practice
   - List any judgmental thoughts present
   - For each judgment, ask:
     * "What need of mine isn't being met?"
     * "What am I longing for?"
   - Rewrite each judgment as a need statement

### Evening Reflection (15-20 minutes)

1. Today's Interactions
   - Note 2-3 significant interactions
   - For each, record:
     * What happened (observations only)
     * How you felt
     * What needs were or weren't met
     * What you learned

2. Gratitude Practice
   - Identify one specific action that enriched your life today
   - Name the feeling it created
   - Acknowledge the need it met

3. Tomorrow's Preparation
   - Note any challenging interactions expected tomorrow
   - Prepare by identifying:
     * Your needs going in
     * Possible needs of others
     * Specific observations you want to make
     * Requests you might make

## Acute Situation Processing

When triggered or in conflict, follow these steps:

### Immediate Response (5-10 minutes)
4. Physical Grounding
   - Find a quiet space
   - Take 5 deep breaths
   - Feel your feet on the ground
   - Notice 3 things you can see, hear, and feel

5. Quick Written Release
   - Write everything you're feeling
   - Don't censor or judge
   - Include body sensations

### Deep Processing (30+ minutes)

6. Observation Clarity
Write down:
   - Exactly what happened (just facts)
   - Specific words spoken
   - Actions taken
   - Time and context

7. Feelings Exploration
   - List all emotions present
   - Rate intensity (1-10)
   - Note physical sensations
   - Track how feelings change as you write

8. Needs Investigation
   - What needs of yours weren't met?
   - What needs might others have been expressing?
   - What needs are universal in this situation?

9. Request Formation
   - What could make life more wonderful?
   - What specific actions could help?
   - How can you make clear, doable requests?

### Integration
After processing, consider:
   - What have you learned?
   - What patterns do you notice?
   - What would you like to do differently?
   - What support might you need?

## Progress Tracking

Keep a monthly log of:
   - Common triggers
   - Frequent unmet needs
   - Successful strategies
   - Areas of growth
   - Challenges to explore

Review this log regularly to notice patterns and celebrate progress.

Remember: This is a practice. Be as compassionate with yourself as you aim to be with others.
