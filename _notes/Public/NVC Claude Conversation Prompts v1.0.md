Use these prompts to initiate supportive conversations about your NVC practice. Copy and paste the relevant prompt, then add your specific situation or practice details.

## Daily Practice Review

"I've completed today's NVC practice and would like to explore it further. Here's what I wrote: [paste your practice content]. I'd particularly like to examine the needs behind my judgments and explore alternative perspectives. Could you help me deepen this reflection?"

## Acute Situation Processing

"I'm working through a difficult situation using NVC. Here's my initial processing: [paste your processing work]. Could you help me ensure I'm separating observations from evaluations and support me in identifying the needs behind my strong feelings?"

## Pattern Recognition

"I've noticed this pattern in my NVC practice: [describe pattern]. Here are some examples from my journal: [paste examples]. Could you help me understand this pattern through the lens of NVC and explore strategies for growth?"

## Request Refinement

"I'm trying to formulate an effective request regarding [situation]. Here's my current version: [paste request]. Could you help me ensure it's specific, doable, and stated in positive action language?"

## Empathy Practice

"I'm struggling to empathize with someone in this situation: [describe situation]. Here's my attempt to guess their feelings and needs: [paste your guesses]. Could you help me explore other possible feelings and needs I might be missing?"

## Self-Empathy Support

"I'm practicing self-empathy regarding [situation]. Here's what I've written: [paste self-empathy work]. Could you offer additional perspectives or help me deepen my self-compassion?"

## Judgment Translation

"I notice I'm holding these judgments: [list judgments]. Here's my attempt to translate them into needs: [paste translations]. Could you help me ensure I'm reaching the core needs and suggest other possible translations?"

## Celebration and Integration

"I'd like to celebrate this success in my NVC practice: [describe success]. Here's what I think contributed to it: [paste your analysis]. Could you help me identify key learnings and ways to build on this progress?"

When using these prompts, remember to:
1. Be specific about what kind of support you're seeking
2. Include relevant context
3. Share your own attempts and insights
4. Be open to exploring different perspectives

The more specific you can be about your practice and what you'd like to explore, the more focused and helpful the conversation can be.
